## [Discussion](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/arkit/arcamera/trackingstate-swift.enum/limited\(_:\)#Discussion)
In this state, the positions and transforms of anchors in the scene (especially detected planes) may not be accurate or consistent from one captured frame to the next.
See the associated [`ARCamera.TrackingState.Reason`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/arkit/arcamera/trackingstate-swift.enum/reason) value for information you can present to the user for improving tracking quality.
