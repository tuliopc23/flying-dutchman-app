# rootMeanSquaredError

## [Discussion](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/createml/mlregressormetrics/rootmeansquarederror#discussion)
The [`rootMeanSquaredError`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/createml/mlregressormetrics/rootmeansquarederror) is calculated by taking the square-root of the average squared distance between the correct and predicted value.
