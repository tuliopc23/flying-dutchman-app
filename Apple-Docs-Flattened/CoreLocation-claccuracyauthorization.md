# CLAccuracyAuthorization
Constants that indicate the level of location accuracy the app has authorization to use.
iOS 2.0+iPadOS 2.0+Mac Catalyst 13.0+macOS 10.6+tvOS 9.0+visionOS 1.0+watchOS 2.0+
