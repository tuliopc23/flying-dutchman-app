# UIControl.ContentVerticalAlignment
Constants for specifying the vertical alignment of content (text and images) in a control.
iOSiPadOSMac CatalysttvOSvisionOS
```
enum ContentVerticalAlignment
```

## [Overview](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/uikit/uicontrol/contentverticalalignment-swift.enum#overview)
You use these constants as the value of the [`contentVerticalAlignment`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/uikit/uicontrol/contentverticalalignment-swift.property) property.
## [Topics](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/uikit/uicontrol/contentverticalalignment-swift.enum#topics)
### [Constants](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/uikit/uicontrol/contentverticalalignment-swift.enum#Constants)
[`case center`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/uikit/uicontrol/contentverticalalignment-swift.enum/center)
Aligns the content vertically in the center of the control.
[`case top`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/uikit/uicontrol/contentverticalalignment-swift.enum/top)
Aligns the content vertically at the top in the control (the default).
[`case bottom`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/uikit/uicontrol/contentverticalalignment-swift.enum/bottom)
Aligns the content vertically at the bottom in the control.
[`case fill`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/uikit/uicontrol/contentverticalalignment-swift.enum/fill)
Aligns the content vertically to fill the content rectangle; images may be stretched.
