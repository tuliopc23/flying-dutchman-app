## [Discussion](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/swiftui/view/glasseffectid\(_:in:\)#discussion)
You use this modifier with the [`glassEffect(_:in:)`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/swiftui/view/glasseffect\(_:in:\)) view modifier and a [`GlassEffectContainer`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/swiftui/glasseffectcontainer) view. When used together, SwiftUI uses the identifier to animate shapes to and from each other during transitions.
