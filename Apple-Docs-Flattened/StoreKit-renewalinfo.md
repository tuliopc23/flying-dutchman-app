## [Overview](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/storekit/product/subscriptioninfo/renewalinfo#overview)
`Product.SubscriptionInfo.RenewalInfo` provides information about the next subscription renewal period. Check the [`state`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/storekit/product/subscriptioninfo/status-swift.struct/state) to determine whether the subscription will be active (subscribed), expired, or in another state at the next renewal period.
